Hi, I’m Yihan Guo

I am a M.S. student in Computational & Applied Mathematics at University of Chicago.
Before coming to Chicago, I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Fudan University in Shanghai.
I am interested in probability theory, and topics where randomness is used as a tool to prove existence and bounds, such as extremal combinatorics, random graphs and hypergraphs, and some problems in theoretical computer science.
I am also interested in the research of geometric and topological objects from a computational viewpoint.
